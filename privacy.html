Last Updated: March 4, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application مسبحة (Masbaha) for mobile devices.

This Application does not collect precise real-time location information of your mobile device.

We do not collect user data; therefore, no information is shared with third parties.

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13.
